Here are the general steps involved in this process of Add, Remove & Replacement of light fixture

The installation, removal, or replacement of light fixtures should be done by a professional electrical contractor.

01
Add a Light Fixture
  • Turn off the power supply to the area where the light fixture will be installed.
  • Determine the location and height of the new light fixture.
  • Install an electrical box in the ceiling or wall at the desired location of the light fixture.
  • Run electrical cable from the nearest power source to the electrical box.
  • Connect the wires from the new light fixture to the wires in the electrical box.
  • Mount the light fixture to the electrical box and secure it in place.
  • Turn on the power supply to the area where the light fixture was installed.
  • Test the new light fixture to ensure that it is working correctly.
02
Remove a Light Fixture
  • Turn off the power supply to the light fixture.
  • Remove the cover or shade of the light fixture.
  • Disconnect the wires from the light fixture and remove the mounting screws.
  • Remove the light fixture from the electrical box.
  • Cap the wires in the electrical box with wire nuts.
  • Turn on the power supply to the area where the light fixture was removed.
03
Replace a Light Fixture
  • Turn off the power supply to the light fixture.
  • Remove the cover or shade of the old light fixture.
  • Disconnect the wires from the old light fixture and remove the mounting screws.
  • Remove the old light fixture from the electrical box.
  • Install a new electrical box, if needed, and connect the wires from the new light fixture to the wires in the electrical box.
  • Mount the new light fixture to the electrical box and secure it in place.
  • Turn on the power supply to the area where the light fixture was replaced.
  • Test the new light fixture to ensure that it is working correctly.
