Ceiling fan repair in Singapore is serviced by AVV Electrical Service, With professional electrician and well experienced professional will give best ceiling fan service in Singapore.

01
Turn off power supply

Before starting any repair work on your ceiling fan, make sure to turn off the power supply to the room or area where the fan is installed.

02
Inspect the fan blades

Check the blades of the fan for any damage or warping that may be causing the fan to wobble or make noise. If any of the blades are damaged, they may need to be replaced.

03
Check the fan motor

Check the fan motor to see if it is running properly. If the motor is not running or is making unusual noises, it may need to be replaced.

04
Inspect the electrical connections

Check the electrical connections between the fan and the ceiling to make sure they are secure and not damaged.

05
Check the remote control (if applicable)

If your ceiling fan is controlled by a remote, check the batteries and make sure they are functioning properly. You may also need to reprogram the remote.

06
Lubricate the fan

If the fan is making a grinding or squeaking noise, it may need to be lubricated. Use a non-conductive lubricant to lubricate the motor and other moving parts

07
Test the fan

Once you’ve completed the repairs, turn the power supply back on and test the fan to make sure it’s working properly.
