The replacement and repair of faulty power points and light switches

Repair or replacement is necessary to avoid hazards, and it’s important to practice electrical safety when dealing with these issues. Always turn off the power to the affected area before attempting any repairs, and consider calling a expert electrician for assistance if you are unsure about the extent of the damage or do not feel comfortable making repairs yourself.

01

Turn off the power supply to the power point or light switch. Remove the cover plate of the power point or light switch.

02

Disconnect the wires from the old power point or light switch. Install the new power point or light switch in place of the old one, making sure it is securely mounted.

03

Reconnect the wires to the new power point or light switch, following the manufacturer’s instructions or as per the standard electrical code.

04

Replace the cover plate of the power point or light switch. Turn on the power supply to the power point or light switch.

05

Test the power point or light switch to ensure that it is working correctly.
