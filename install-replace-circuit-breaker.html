Here are some general steps for circuit breaker installation and services

Circuit breaker installation and services are crucial tasks that involve installing, maintaining, and repairing electrical panels or circuit breaker boxes. These boxes control and distribute electricity to different parts of a building, and proper installation and maintenance are essential for safety and efficient operation.

01

Turn off the main power supply to the distribution board. Identify which circuit breakers or sub-circuits are to be connected to the new DB box.

02

Remove the cover of the existing DB box, and disconnect the cables from the circuit breakers or sub-circuits to be moved to the new DB box. Remove the existing DB box from the wall, and prepare the new DB box for installation.

03

Install the new DB box onto the wall, making sure it is level and securely mounted. Connect the cables from the circuit breakers or sub-circuits to the appropriate terminals in the new DB box.

04

Install the new circuit breakers into the new DB box, making sure they are correctly rated for the load and type of circuit.

05

Replace the cover of the new DB box, and restore the power supply to the distribution board.Test the new circuit breakers and sub-circuits to ensure that they are working correctly.
